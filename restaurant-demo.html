Our Story

Cooking as an
Act of Love

Born from a single wood-fired kitchen in the hills, Ember & Ash was founded on the belief that great food is an act of devotion — to ingredients, to guests, and to the ancient art of fire.

We source exclusively from farmers and artisans within 100 miles. Every morning begins at the market. Every evening ends with a table full of stories.

👨‍🍳
Marcus Delacroix
Executive Chef & Founder
