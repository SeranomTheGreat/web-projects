Our Story

Where every dish tells a story

VELOUR was born from a singular conviction — that dining is theatre, and every guest deserves the finest act of their evening.

We source ingredients at the peak of their season, partner with local farmers and artisans, and transform them through classical French technique with a distinctly regional soul.
