Our Story

Cooking with
Fire & Passion

Founded in 2015, Ember & Ash was born from a simple belief — that the best meals are cooked slowly, with intention. Our wood-fired ovens burn for 14 hours a day.

🌾

Locally Sourced

Every ingredient comes within 80km of Imphal. We work directly with 12 partner farms.

🔥

Wood-Fired Always

Our signature applewood kiln gives every dish an irreplaceable depth of flavour.

👨‍🍳

Mastercraft Chefs

Led by Executive Chef Rajan Thoudam, trained across Delhi, Kolkata, and Bangkok.
