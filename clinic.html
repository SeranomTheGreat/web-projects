What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

From preventive care to advanced treatment, our multidisciplinary team covers all your health needs under one roof.

🫀

Cardiology

Expert heart health assessment, ECG, stress testing, and management of cardiac conditions.

🧠

Neurology

Diagnosis and treatment of neurological conditions including migraines, epilepsy, and stroke.

🦴

Orthopaedics

Bone, joint and muscle care — from sports injuries to arthritis management and physiotherapy.

👁️

Ophthalmology

Complete eye care services — vision testing, cataract diagnosis, and retina examinations.

🧪

Diagnostics & Lab

In-house pathology, blood work, X-ray, ultrasound — fast results, accurate readings.

👶

Paediatrics

Dedicated care for infants, children and adolescents — vaccinations, growth monitoring and more.
