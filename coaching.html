FAQs

What batch timings are available? ▼
Morning (7–9 AM), Afternoon (2–4 PM), and Evening (5–8 PM) batches available on weekdays. Weekend-only batches also offered.
Do you offer online classes? ▼
Yes! All courses are available in both offline (classroom) and fully online modes via live interactive sessions. Recorded lectures are included in all plans.
Is there an EMI option available? ▼
Absolutely. We offer no-cost EMI plans — typically split into 3 or 4 instalments over the course duration. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
Are study materials included? ▼
Yes. All courses include comprehensive printed study notes, formula booklets, past year papers, and access to our digital test-series platform — at no extra cost.
